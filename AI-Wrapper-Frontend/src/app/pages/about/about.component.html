About CSAGE-BOT

Revolutionizing How You Interact with Information

Our Mission

In a world overflowing with data, our mission is to empower students, researchers, and professionals to cut through the noise. We believe that understanding shouldn't be a chore. PDF-AI was built to transform dense documents into digestible insights, fostering quicker learning and more efficient research.

What We Do

Using state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP) and deep learning models, our platform provides two core functions:

  • AI-Powered Summarization: Upload any PDF and receive a concise, accurate summary that captures the key arguments and data points.
  • Intelligent Question Generation: Go beyond summarization. Our AI generates critical thinking questions based on the document's content, perfect for study guides, meeting preparations, or deepening your own understanding.

The Visionaries

We are a passionate team of AI researchers, software engineers, and user experience designers based right here in Telangana, India. We are dedicated to building intuitive tools that leverage the power of artificial intelligence to solve real-world challenges in education and productivity.
